  • TRIP ITINERARY



  • Day 01 - Arrival Delhi | By Flight: Own Arrangements | No Meals


    On your arrival at Delhi international airport, welcome by our representative & you will be transferred to Hotel. Check in at hotel. Day free for own activities. Overnight stay in Delhi.



  • Day 02 - Delhi – Agra | By Surface: 233 Kms / 04 Hrs | Breakfast


    After breakfast straight drive to Agra. Upon arrival check in at Hotel. Relax a while. Afternoon proceeds for the sightseeing of Agra visiting Taj Mahal & Agra Fort. After sightseeing of Agra return to hotel. Overnight stay will be at Agra Hotel.



  • Day 03 - Agra – Ftehpur Sikri – Jaipur | By Surface: 232 Kms/5 .5 Hrs | Breakfast


    Breakfast at hotel. Morning free for leisure. In the evening proceed on Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner, for giving you the real time of adventure, we offer you our best and exciting desert safari package by which you will have a chance to enjoy magnificent landscape of unique Arabian sands. By joining us, you will get a chance of being present into the heart of the desert where excitement of a roller-coaster tour. Breathtaking views of desert would be ready to be captured in your camera and you will enjoy astonishing scenes of endless desert. Don’t miss the chance of watching sun-set of this tranquil scenery. Your Desert Safari Tours will be truly wonderful and will be an unforgettable time you will spend in UAE. After the tour transfer to hotel and overnight staying at the hotel.



  • Day 04 - Jaipur | By Surface: Local Run | Breakfast


    Morning after early breakfast proceed for the sightseeing of Jaipur visiting Amer Fort, Jal Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar & Hawa Mahal. After Jaipur sightseeing returns to hotel for Overnight Stay.

  • Day 05 - Jaipur – Delhi | By Surface: 262 Kms/262 Kms| Breakfast


    Morning after breakfast straight drive to Delhi. Upon arrival at Delhi you can visit Local market places of Delhi OR Delhi Local Sightseeing Visit Qutub Minar, Humayun Tomb, Lotus Temple, India Gate, after which proceed to visit drive pass PRESIDENT HOUSE & PARLIAMENT HOUSE. On arrival Delhi transfer to your hotel check in at Hotel & Relax. Overnight Stay.

  • Day 06 - Delhi Departure | By Flight: Own Arrangements


    After according to your flight time schedules our company representative will transfer you to the Delhi Airport to connect your flight for onward destination with the sweet memories of Golden Triangle Tour. We wish you a safe and pleasant journey home on behalf of OUR TEAM!!!
