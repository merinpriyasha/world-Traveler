    Breakfast at hotel, after breakfast proceed on Full Day Sunway Lagoon Tour, it provides the ultimate theme park experience in 6 adventure zones — Water Park, Amusement Park, Wildlife Park, Extreme Park, Scream Park & Nickelodeon Lost Lagoon.
