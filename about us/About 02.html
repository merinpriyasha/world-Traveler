About World Traveler..




Operating as a customer-centric company, Travel Data puts our clients needs and requirements as our first priority.
Being an innovative leader in the travel sector for over 30 years, Travel Data has moulded a reputation of
trust and reliability with all our clients.

Over the years, our company has expanded to meet the requirements of our growing host of clients,
and now has a workforce of over 30 professional employees,
across all our growing portfolio of business units, such as air ticketing, visa assistance,
inbound and outbound tour operations, travel insurance and event management.

Operating within global travel sector standards,
World Traveler has been a proud recipient of numerous accolades and awards,
including membership of the International Air Transport Association (IATA).We pride ourselves of nurturing long-term strategic
partnerships to ensure we offer global standards and the widest resources to our valuable clients.
