  • Day 01:Arrival

  • Day 02: Half Day Dubai City Tour + Dhow Dinner Creek Cruise (B/D)


    Breakfast at hotel, after breakfast proceed on Dubai City Tour,
    The Dubai City Tour is the perfect way to discover this magnificent city.
    You’ll be taken on a guided tour around Dubai, getting the opportunity to
    see and learn about many of the iconic landmarks and destinations. The tour
    will showcase Dubai’s stature as a global city with diverse offerings of culture, cuisine and attractions. This 5-hour tour takes you around the metropolis in an air-conditioned vehicle. It gives you an opportunity for sightseeing many of the architectural wonders and cultural elements of the city. Visit the world’s tallest building – Burj Khalifa, the most magnificent man-made structure which is similar to the orientation of a desert flower named Hymenocallis, AKA Spider Lily., the Burj al Arab, the Atlantis resort, the Palm Jumeirah, the Sheikh’s Palace, and more. You’ll also stop by the Dubai Museum, the famous Dubai Gold Souk, and Jumeirah Mosque, located in the historical district of Dubai. There will be photo stops along the tour so there is plenty of time to capture special moments or just soak in the amazing sights. And proceed on Dhow Cruise with Dinner. After transfer to hotel and overnight staying at the hotel.



  • Day 03: Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner (B/D)


    Breakfast at hotel. Morning free for leisure. In the evening proceed on Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner, for giving you the real time of adventure, we offer you our best and exciting desert safari package by which you will have a chance to enjoy magnificent landscape of unique Arabian sands. By joining us, you will get a chance of being present into the heart of the desert where excitement of a roller-coaster tour. Breathtaking views of desert would be ready to be captured in your camera and you will enjoy astonishing scenes of endless desert. Don’t miss the chance of watching sun-set of this tranquil scenery. Your Desert Safari Tours will be truly wonderful and will be an unforgettable time you will spend in UAE. After the tour transfer to hotel and overnight staying at the hotel.
