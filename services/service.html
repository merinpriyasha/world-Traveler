Visa Assistance

Immigration procedures, requirements and processes are ever-changing. Our visa consultants at Travel Data keep updated on the latest rules and regulations. We extend our visa services for all countries and for all visa types, including expats and migration. Additionally, our services include form submission, passport services, translations & document attestation, and document delivery to minimise your hassle of acquiring visa.



Air Ticketing Solutions

As a proud accredited member of the International Air Transport Association(IATA), Travel Data offers ticketing solutions to both end-clients, as well as to non-IATA accredited travel companies. As part of our sub-agent ticketing solutions to non-IATA accredited travel companies, we offer a 24/7 call centre service, ensuring your ticketing requirements are met efficiently and promptly.



Foreign Currency Exchange

Travel Data offers in-house foreign currency exchange services through our partner Data Exchange International (Pvt) Ltd, which is a member of the Data Group of Companies. As a fully licensed foreign exchange company in Sri Lanka, we strive you give you the very best rates available in the market.
