  • TRIP ITINERARY



  • Day 01: ARRIVAL SINGAPORE


    Upon arrive at Singapore Changi Airport, Meet and Greet with our friendly Tour Guide then transfer to Hotel for check-in and overnight at hotel.



  • Day 02: NIGHT SAFARI (B)


    Breakfast at the hotel. Thereafter pickup from hotel lobby then proceed with Night Safari Tour. The world’s first Night Safari*, set in 40 hectares of secondary jungle, will unfold the mystery and drama of the tropical jungle after dusk with stunning effects. See a myriad of nocturnal animals, from fierce predators to timid forest dwellers, in their natural habitat under subtle moong low lighting. With priority boarding, you won’t have to wait in line to board the tram which takes you through the East and West Loop. Pass by a large reservoir and weave through selected habitats designed specifically to replicate the natural environment from the Himalayan Foothills to the Southeast Asian Rainforest and Indian Subcontinent. Let the guide take you on a walking trail to view some animals at close quarters. There will also be an opportunity to catch either the Thumbuakar performance of fire-eating skills OR a 20mins animal extravaganza at the Creatures of the Night Show, subject to weather conditions.



  • Day 03: FREE PROGRAM (B)


    Breakfast at hotel. Free for leisure y own. Overnight staying at the hotel.



  • Day 04: DEPARTURE (B)


    Breakfast at hotel. Will pick up at Hotel Lobby for transfer out with nice memories.
